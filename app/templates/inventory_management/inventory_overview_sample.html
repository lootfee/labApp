Please login and create a company or affiliate yourself to one in order to access this page.

  1. Register and login.
  2. Click on your username.
  3. Click on edit profile and update your user data.
  4. Affiliate yourself to a company.
    1. Search for company's name.
    2. Click request to affiliate to company.
    3. Wait for the admin of the company to accept your request.
    4. Once accepted, your title/role and affiliated company will show on your user profile page.
    5. Click on the company name to go to company profile page.
    6. Once in the company page, click on Inventory Management tab at the top of the page to acces company inventory software.
  5. If the company is not yet registered on labApp, click on register a company.
    1. Type your company's name, click submit.
    2. It will take you to the company profile page
    3. Click on edit/update company profile and fill up the details and save.
    4. Click on Admin tab at the top of the page.
    5. Find your username and click edit role.
    6. Assign yourself a title and click save.
    7. Your title and company will now be visible on your profile page.
    8. Click on the company name to go to company profile page.
    9. Once in the company page, click on Inventory Management tab at the top of the page to acces company inventory software.

Inventory Management guide (for company admins)

Please follow as per the order of instructions.

  1. On company profile page, click on Inventory Management at the top of the page.
  2. Once inside the Inventory Management page, click on Departments tab.
    1. Register supplies department name (e.g. Biochemistry, Hematology, Microbiology, etc.).
    2. Some basic departments are auto completed.
  3. Click on Suppliers tab.
    1. Click on register supplier.
    2. Fill out supplier details.
  4. Click on Supplies tab.
    1. Click on register supplies.
    2. Type product reference number.
    3. If product is already registered in our database, reference number will show.
    4. Click on the reference number to auto populate item name, description and storage requirement and type.
    5. Fill out other details as per your company's data and click register.
    6. Do this for all your company's inventory supplies.
