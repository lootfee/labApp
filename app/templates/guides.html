Getting started

  • Calculators and Quality Control can be used without signing in.
  • To use other services, please sign up and login.
  • After signing in you will be taken to the Home Page, you can access your Home Page by clicking the labApp logo on top left.
  • In the Home Page is where you can create posts ,view your post and posts of the users you follow.
  • In the Explore page is where you can see posts of other users that you do not follow.
  • To like/unlike or comment on a post, click the body of the post to redirect you to the post page.
  • To update/view your user profile, click on your username at the top-right of the page.

Creating a profile for your company.

  • If you are an owner or admin of a Clinical Laboratory, you can create a profile page for your company.
  • You will then have access to the built in Inventory Manager Software within labApp.
  • To register a company, go to your users profile by clicking your username at the top-right of the page.
  • Click on register a company, and fill up the company profile form.
  • After successfully registering a company, it will take you to the company profile page.
  • Click on the Admin tab at the top center.
  • In the Admin tab is where you can manage your affiliate roles and titles as well and accept users that requests to affiliate to your company.
  • Look for your username and click Edit Role.
  • Enter your title on the company and click save. (Do not remove the check on the Super Admin!)
  • Do this for other users that you have accepted as your affiliates. (A user must be assigned a role so the affiliated company will show on their profile. )
  • Your role and affiliated company can now be seen on your users profile.

Affiliating to a company.

  • If you are an employee of a company that is registered on labApp, you can access its built in softwares within labApp by affiliating your user profile to the company profile.
  • Search for the name of the company on the search bar and click on it.
  • Once on the company profile page, click on request to affiliate to company.
  • Wait for the admin of the company to accept your request.
  • Once accepted and given a role, your affiliated company and role will be visible on your users profile.

Using the Inventory Management Software.

Please follow as per the order of instructions.

  1. On company profile page, click on Inventory Management at the top of the page.
  2. Once inside the Inventory Management page, click on Departments tab.
    1. Register supplies department name (e.g. Biochemistry, Hematology, Microbiology, Phlebotomy etc.).
    2. Some basic departments are auto completed.
    3. For smaller laboratories that only have a few number of supplies and only 1 or 2 persons rotating on all departments, you can use a not so specific department name(e.g General, Lab Supplies, etc.).
  3. Click on Suppliers tab.
    1. Click on register supplier.
    2. Fill out supplier details.
  4. Click on Supplies tab (To be done by Inventory Admin/User who created the company on labapp.tech ).
    1. Click on register supplies.
    2. Type product reference number.
    3. If product is already registered in our database, reference number will show.
    4. Click on the reference number to auto populate item name, description and storage requirement and type.
    5. Fill out other details as per your company's data and click register.
    6. Do this for all your company's inventory supplies.
    7. If you have multiple suppliers on a single item, you can click the edit(pencil) button and set the item you don't usually use a inactive.
  5. Go back to the Departments page and click link supplies on the side of the department name.
    1. Choose the item that you want to link to the certain department (this will be used for querying supplies).

Process of ordering supplies (use this same process when encoding your initial inventory).

  1. Go to Orders tab and click on Create New Order tab on top left of the page.
  2. Choose which department to link the order and click submit.
  3. Order number is auto-generated based on your company abbreviation, department and date.
  4. Click on add items on top left to add items to your order.
  5. Click save.
  6. You can still add items on the order if it still not submitted.
  7. If all the items you wish to order is saved, click on submit.
  8. For Inventory Admins.
    1. On the Order Status column, Create Purchase Order tab will show.
    2. Click on Create Purchase Order tab to create purchase order.
    3. Purchase Order number is auto generated, click Create.
    4. On the Purchases tab, your Purchase Order Number will show.
    5. Click on Purchase Order Number to view or edit the order.
    6. Once submitted, the Purchase Order Number can be viewed on the Deliveries tab.
  9. If supplies have been delivered, click on Accept Delivery.
  10. Choose the supplier name and enter the Delivery Order Number.
  11. It will then take you to the Accept Delivery page.
  12. Choose on the items that were delivered by clicking receive.
  13. Enter the lot number, expiry date and quantity of items received.
  14. If all items were delivered, the actions tab will show Delivery Complete.
  15. If delivered items were not complete, click on Accept Delivery again to encode a new Delivery Order Number once a new delivery is made.
  16. Delivered items will now show on your Inventory page.
  17. Expiry date of items that are below the minimum expiry that was set will be marked red.
  18. On the Overview page, Reagents that are less than minimum quantity, near expiry, undelivered reagents will be visible.
  19. Submitted orders, Pending purchases and Pending deliveries will be visible for inventory admins as well.

Transferring supplies to different departments (Also for use by those that have centralised inventory system.)

  1. Navigate to Internal tab.
  2. Click create request on the top right.
  3. Choose the requesting department and the department where you would like to request the supplies and click submit.
  4. Click on add items on the top left and the list of supplies on each department will be shown.
  5. Click on the Qty request column and enter the quantity that you would like to request and click add.
  6. Click on save to save your request.
  7. Inventory supervisors can then submit the request once it is complete.
  8. Your newly created request will now be shown on the internal page.
  9. (For inventory supervisors)To transfer the requested supplies:
    1. Click on the request ID in the internal page to take you to the request list.
    2. On the transfer qty column, enter the quantity you wish to transfer and click the transfer button.
